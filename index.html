About

Hello, my name is Tynon. I am a software developer with skills in JavaScript, React.js, and Node.js.

I love creating applications and features that make clients sing along with the skills to improve existing applications to better fit client expectations. It is more difficult than the client facing side but the power you feel being able to think of an idea then create it is amazing. I will admit my passion is pretty broad, I love programming to get an application to complete a task that I find difficult or tedious. Please connect with me with the social media links at the bottom of the page or using the contact form!

Before becoming a software developer, I worked as a software support specialist in the healthcare, financial, and software industries. During this time, I honed my skills in client and internal communication, acting as the vital 'oil' that kept operations running smoothly. My exposure to both positive and critical client feedback on products sparked my interest in coding and motivated me to transition into software development.
